Spaghetti Bolognese

Serves 4. Prep 15 min. Cook 45 min.

Ingredients

  • 500g minced beef
  • 400g spaghetti
  • 1 can chopped tomatoes
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic
  • Salt, pepper, olive oil

Instructions

  1. Brown the beef in olive oil.
  2. Add onion and garlic, cook until soft.
  3. Add tomatoes, simmer 30 minutes.
  4. Cook spaghetti al dente, combine and serve.
